Information

A leading independent public school providing a stimulating environment where learning flourishes and our students are given the skills and education to build their future upon. Our logo envisions all we would hope our Wembley Primary School students to be. The lion represents ‘moral courage’ which by definition is the tenacity to take action and speak up for what is right despite the risk of adverse consequences. At Wembley Primary School whilst encouraging our students to step out with ‘moral courage’ we help them to understand that their intention must first and foremost be underpinned by the core values of respect, empathy and service. ‘Courage is required to take action when one has doubts or fears about the consequences’
